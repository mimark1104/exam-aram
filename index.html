Why Orsus

Orsus is a free decentralized platform that leverages both traditional and blockchain based smart contracts to bring transparency and immutability into the talent discovery process.
Fans and followers can use the Orsus platform to discover, collaborate with, and participate in the revenue successes of new stars without the need for traditional industry gatekeepers.

The Orsus ecosystem

Orsuschain

A proprietary hybrid chain that uses PoS and connects with a public chain enabling instant, secure, low cost transactions.

Orxchange

A built-in exchange where fans, emerging talents and leading creators can trade tokens and fund projects in a vibrant secondary market.

DApps

An open platform featuring both native and free plug-ins for any third party DApps that support the tokenization of IP monetization and talent discovery.

Orsus empowers fans to use their voice, crowd fund art projects, vote on talent shows, and gain financial rewards
